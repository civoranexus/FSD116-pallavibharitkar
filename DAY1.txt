Day 1 – Project Planning and Requirement Analysis

Project Name:
Nursery & Seed Management System

Internship Program:
CivoraX Internship Program – Civora Nexus Pvt. Ltd.

Objective of Day 1:
To understand the project requirements, identify user roles, and study the main modules before starting development.

User Roles Identified:
Admin – Manages the system, users, and reports
Nursery Staff – Handles inventory, batch details, and plant health
Customer – Views plants and places orders

Core Modules Studied:
User authentication and role-based access
Inventory and stock management
Batch and life cycle tracking
Sales and order management
Procurement and supplier management
Administration and reporting

Outcome of Day 1:
Understood the project scope clearly
Identified system users and modules
Ready to start technical planning and design
